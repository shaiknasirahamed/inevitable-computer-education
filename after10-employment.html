Private Jobs (Common Entry Roles)

  • Office Assistant / Front Office
  • Computer Operator / Data Entry (basic computer skills)
  • Sales / Retail / Store Assistant
  • Delivery / Support roles (based on company requirements)
  • Technician helper roles (good if you do ITI/Diploma)
Tip: Doing ITI/Diploma + English + basic computer skills increases salary options.

Government Jobs (AP & Karnataka) – Official Websites

  • APPSC (Andhra Pradesh PSC): https://portal-psc.ap.gov.in/portal-psc.ap.gov.in
  • AP Police Recruitment (SLPRB): https://slprb.ap.gov.in/slprb.ap.gov.in
  • KPSC (Karnataka PSC): https://kpsc.kar.nic.in/kpsc.kar.nic.in
  • Karnataka Police Recruitment Page: https://ksp.karnataka.gov.in/info-3/Recruitment/enksp.karnataka.gov.in
Always check eligibility carefully (age/education/physical standards). Use only official portals.
